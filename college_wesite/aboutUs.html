Why study at WeDoCode

​​​​​​​​​​​​ ​​​​​​​​​​​ For over 10 years WeDoCode, a brand of the Independent Institute of Education since 2005, has delivered quality tertiary education to students across South Africa. Situated in bustling cities, IIE WeDoCode embraces the same vibrant environment which allows over 18 000 students to connect with the heart of the institution.​

Credible Institution

The Independent Institute of Education’s quality, accredited qualifications have a strong technological fo​cus, enabling students to thrive in today’s working world. You can choose to study part-time (contact), full-time (contact) or through distance /online learning - at the Bloemfontein, Cape Town, Polokwane, Pretoria CBD, Pretoria Sunnyside, Pietermaritzburg, Port Elizabeth or Durban campus.​

Accredited Independent Institute of Education’s Qualifications

The institution's academic approach is adamantly career orientated and technology-driven. The Independent Institute of Education’s qualifications are designed and frequently reviewed to meet changing market demands. This guarantees that qualifications offered are in line with the latest skill developments within a specific career field and that students have the opportunity to embark on higher education studies which assist them to achieve success, both academically and professionally. WeDoCode is characterised by a secure and vibrant campus life.

Educators W​ho Care​

Not only does The Independent Institute of Education’s, through WeDoCode, prepare aduates with the theory they need to be successful in their career paths, they also prepare the graduates for employment.
